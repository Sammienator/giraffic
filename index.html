About Giraffic

Giraffic World is a product design and marketing solutions company established in 2012 by its founder and C.E.O Kimanthi Eugene. In 2022, Mr kimanthi patnered with Mr, Telvin Mwangi (C.C.P) as the Chief company photographer as well as Mr. Samuel Mugo, C.E.O of Sammienator Inc, and Communications Technology Officer at Giraffic World.

Mr Kimanthi has always been passionate about the world of design, art and digital print.His motivation and drive for this path led to the need to provide a platform for him to showcase his abilities and thus led to the creation of giraffic world!

Giraffic world is an online platform dedicated to showcasing art and culture as seen through the eyes of Mr, Kimanthi. He also features an online gallery reserved for branded merchandise,art purchases and general consultancy services.
